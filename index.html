Exploratory Visualization of Survey Data

This exploratory visualization lets you see the responses for a particular question and compare it with responses to a different question, both as a proportion of total responses to the questions. The facility to juxtapose any question with any other question enables endless ways to slice and dice the data, leading to a nuanced understanding the responses.

We have only considered responses from 31 questions which are closed questions of the 50 questions from the survey, deliberately leaving out the responses from the 19 open-ended questions. We have classified the 31 questions in 5 broad categories so they can be easily located and selected.

About the Data

The analysis and visualization is made from datasets from the Data Visualization Society.
